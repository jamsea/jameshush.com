Written Bio

With nearly a decade of AdTech and publishing experience, James Hush has led or been part of some of the fastest, most punctual, engineering teams, including System1, GoGuardian, and NFL Media—the latter which he deployed live features on SuperBowl.com during the actual Super Bowl.

As his days as Lead Engineer for Remo consisted of daily deployments for worldwide events, James was the goto Punctual Engineer when it came to delivering features across teams on time and under budget. LONGER VERSION
The Punctual Engineer

Overworked software engineers have to deal with broken products and unmet expectations on a daily basis. Lead Engineer James Hush shows that we already have the time to do proactive, long term work as engineers—no matter the size of the company.

After this keynote, your engineer- and executive-filled audience will be able to:

  • Identify 8 No-Crash Steps for testing deployments that save time, money, and Advil.
  • Discover an easier way of thinking of your career in the long term (and why it helps, even with a smattering of short-term problems).
  • Dissect why Fridays are the most important work day of the week and how it can save clients millions of dollars.
  • Connect with stakeholders and communicate how to deploy new updates on time (and sometimes early).
  • Pinpoint a plan to never miss a Thanksgiving dinner (or Chinese New Year) with family again.
